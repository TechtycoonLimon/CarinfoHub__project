About Us

CarinfoHub is your go-to platform for car information in Bangladesh. We provide detailed specs, prices, and pictures of cars available in the Bangladeshi market, focusing on popular reconditioned and used models from brands like Toyota, Honda, and Nissan. With the car market in Bangladesh heavily reliant on imports and reconditioned vehicles due to high import taxes (often 100-200% on new cars), we aim to help you make informed decisions. Our data is sourced from trusted platforms like Bikroy, Bhalogari, and market trends reported by Bangladesh Road Transport Authority.
